Sir Winston Churchill

Winston Leonard Spencer-Churchill (30 November 1874 – 24 January 1965)

A little about Winstone.

He was a British politician, army officer, and writer. He was the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom from 1940 to 1945, when he led Britain to victory in the Second World War.

Mixed English and American parentage, Churchill was born in Oxfordshire to a wealthy, aristocratic family

One of Sir Winstones greatest quotes is... Continuous effort – not strength or intelligence – is the key to unlocking our potential.
